An assortment of sources that I particularly enjoy and tend to peruse regularly.

Aggregators

  • Hacker News - Technology-focused news aggregator run by Y Combinator that often features interesting reader commentary
  • KDnuggets - Machine learning content aggregator with some original content.

News

  • Block Club Chicago - The best local journalism source.

Academic

  • Distill - Machine learning research journal with a new, modern web-based medium of presentation.
  • Quanta Magazine - Publication focused on distilling scientific news and discoveries.

Blogs

  • Marginal Revolution - Economic news, analysis, and cultural commentary.
  • Curbed - Real estate news.
  • Eater - Restaurant news and coverage.
  • Discover Dev - Technology-focused daily link roundup.
  • Multithreaded - The Stitch Fix engineering team blog.
  • Towards Data Science - Platform for data science articles.
