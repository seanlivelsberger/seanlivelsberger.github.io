An assortment of sources that I browse regularly.

Aggregators

  • Hacker News - Technology-focused news aggregator run by Y Combinator.
  • KDnuggets - Machine learning content aggregator.

News

  • The Economist - The best journalism out there.
  • Block Club Chicago - The best local journalism.

Academic

  • Distill - Machine learning research journal with a modernized form of presentation.
  • Quanta Magazine - Publication focused on distilling scientific news and discoveries.

Blogs

  • Marginal Revolution - Economic news, analysis, and cultural commentary.
  • Bloomberg Opinion - Unique perspectives on the world.
  • Curbed - Real estate news.
  • Eater - Restaurant news and coverage.
  • Discover Dev - Technology-focused daily link roundup.
  • Multithreaded - The Stitch Fix engineering team blog.
  • Towards Data Science - Platform for data science articles.

Newsletters

  • Data Science Weekly - Weekly newsletter featuring curated news, articles and jobs related to data science.
  • Data Elixir - Weekly newsletter covering the top tools and trends in data science.
  • O'Reilly Data Newsletter - Weekly industry updates.
  • Review - Weekly publication from First Round Capital featuring content focused on startups and venture capital.
  • PyTorch Newsletter - Latest in machine learning development from PyTorch and Facebook.
