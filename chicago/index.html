I've lived in or around Chicago my whole life. Below are a some of my favorite parts of the city.

  • First Ascent - Climbing gym.
  • Bang Bang Pie & Biscuits - Top notch pie and biscuit shop.
  • City Grange - Local garden shop with great selection and community involvement.
  • Gene's Sausage Shop - European-style grocer with an amazing rooftop beer garden.
  • Music Box Theatre - Some of the best programming in town with a some of the most fun cult midnight movies.
  • Lincoln Hall - Best concert venue in town. Most modern, cleanliest, and air-conditioned venue. Features a nice selection of up and coming artists.
  • Thalia Hall - Concert venue in a renovated opera house from the 19th Century that features a high quality selection of artsts.
  • Little Goat Diner - Best value restaurant in town. All dishes are memorable, but the chili is a standout.
  • Winnemac Park - Hidden gem of a park on the northside.
  • Au Cheval - Best burger.
  • Kuma's Corner - Second best burger.
  • Lakeshore - Best place to get out on a bike.
  • High Five Ramen - Best ramen.
  • Cemitas Puebla - Underrated mexican restaurant. Amazing cemitas and greasy chips.
  • La Sirena Clandestina - One of my favorite restaurants. Latin American style food with outstanding drinks.
  • The Publican - My favorite restaurant. Top notch meat and vegetables with a truly remarkable beer list.
  • Lou Malnati's - Best deep dish pizza.
  • Piece Brewery and Pizzeria - Best thin crust pizza.
  • Piccolo Sogno - Best Italian.
  • Chicago Q - Best BBQ.
  • Chicago Cut Steakhouse - Best Steakhouse.
  • Sepia - Classy joint with amazing food.
  • Next - A top dining experience. Rotating world-class menus.
  • Alinea - The most memorable dining experience.
  • Prost! - Best German beer bar.
  • El Ideas - One of the most fun and creative fine dining experiences. And it is amazingly BYOB!
  • Valois - My favorite restaurant in Hyde Park.
  • Al's Beef - Best Italian beef.
  • The University of Chicago Main Quad - Beautiful place with Gothic Architecture.
  • Montrose Beach - The break wall is a cool place to walk out to with a great view. Otherwise a great beach and place to paddleboard.
  • Pitchfork Music Festival - Pitchfork sponsored music festival with great indie bands. Best festival vibe.
  • Promontory Point - One of the best views of the skyline on the lakefront.
  • Statue of the Republic - Hidden gem of a statue in the style of the Columbian Exposition.
